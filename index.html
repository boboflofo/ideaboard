Spotify Clone

Functionality

  • In this spotify clone, users will be able to play their desired music with a music player, with functionality such as pausing, playing, and volume adjustments
  • Users will be able to use their own spotify login to access their spotify information
  • This application will utilize the spotify api to receive information of playlists and other data from the user login
  • Users can also choose their own playlists to play, or create a personalized playlist for future use
  • Spotify clone will have a functional music player
  • The design of the application will look like that of spotify
